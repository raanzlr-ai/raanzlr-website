# Raanzlr Company Info

Raanzlr is an AI automation and software engineering agency building AI
agents & chatbots, workflow automation, web platforms, mobile apps, systems
integration, and custom AI solutions. It serves teams across the GCC, MENA,
Türkiye, Europe, and the United States, in Arabic, English, and Turkish.

## How to get information

1. **Structured summary:** fetch `https://raanzlr.com/llms.txt` for a
   machine-readable overview of the company, services, and key pages.
2. **Services:** browse `https://raanzlr.com/en/services` (or `/ar/services`
   for Arabic). Each service has a detail page at
   `https://raanzlr.com/en/services/<slug>` — slugs: `ai-chatbots`,
   `workflow-automation`, `web-development`, `mobile-apps`, `custom-ai`,
   `crm-integration`, `ui-ux`, `consulting`.
3. **Markets and industries:** `https://raanzlr.com/en/markets` and
   `https://raanzlr.com/en/industries`.
4. **All indexable pages:** `https://raanzlr.com/sitemap.xml`.

## How to contact Raanzlr

- Email: **info@raanzlr.com** (replies within one business day)
- Contact form: `https://raanzlr.com/en/contact` (protected by Cloudflare
  Turnstile — automated form submission is not supported; use email instead)
